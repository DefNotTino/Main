Outdoors/Indoors

Please listen to the audio before proceeding!

Santino Santos (SJSU Spring 2021)

Created using: Adobe Audtion, Adobe Premiere, Audacity

This project was designed to teach us how to work on video in reverse! Meaning that instead of working on video (typically with the sound included in the recording process), our class had to record the sound first, and then record two contrasting videos to match the audio. Below are the results of the project. This was also inspired by the Surrealism movement and the fantasy, errie vibes that movement displayed in its artworks.

This video was created first, with the setting based on being indoors. During most of the 2020 - 2021 year has kept people locked within our homes due to the COVID-19 outbreak. You can see my process of how I deal with this lockdown.

Lastly, I created this video to match only the audio and not the first video I created (Indoors). The video represents my thoughts of the outside world after being kept in so long. Come dive into my mind!
