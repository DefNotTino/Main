Rain (clone cover)

Santino Santos (SJSU Spring 2021)

Created using Adobe Photoshop, Phone Recorder, Adobe Audition and Audacity

Wrapping up the Spring 2021 semester, Definitely Not Tino and Tino make a kick-ass guitar cover!

This was the final project created for Art 75. In this project, I demonstrate the process of video duplication on Adobe Premiere to create a "clone stamp" effect. In this video I also demonstrate my love for music and combining that art with video production. The video is NOT the original video and includes many cuts from the original version. The song is Rain by Rob Scallon.
